Train Your Learning Outcome

(TYLO)

We train students and teachers to improve the way they understand and deliver the content.

Vision

To collaboratively create visually engaging Subject Contents mapped to Learning Outcomes of NCERT and providing training in all ICT tools to enhance the effectiveness of teaching learning process

Mission

  • To make the Collaboration of Content Creators and Subject Experts across the Country Possible
  • To create Best in the Class Presentations/Animations/Videos for School Teachers readily available for Online Teaching
  • To create Best in the Class Assessment Questions/Methods and Tools readily available for Online Teaching
  • To train the Teachers to present the contents and use the tools for Effective Online and Offline Teaching
  • To improve the contents by obtaining Periodic Evaluation and Feedback from the teachers
  • To obtain feedback from students by conducting periodic online formative assessments
