About

ICAICISD aims to bring leading researchers, academicians, students, and delegates from industries together to discuss the latest advancements and their ideas in the area of AI and CI for Sustainable Development. It is a great platform for researchers, academicians to share their innovation and to gain knowledge in various challenges and solutions adopted in the field of AI.
