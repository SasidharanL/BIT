Welcome to NCIIT

The Computer Science and Telecommunications have been used to occur innovations in IT. The most promising research directions and impacts such innovation might have on society. Consistent themes emerge from this conference. The aim of this conference is to provide an opportunity for experts from Academic Institutions and Industries to discuss and share their experiences, strategy and vision on the research activities in the field of Information Technology. Eminent Scientists and Engineers will also be invited to deliver special lectures. The deliberation of conference will be through presentation of papers and invited talks.

Objectives of NCIIT

The following are the primary objectives of the conference:

  • To facilitate interaction and knowledge exchange among academia, research organizations and industry.
  • To deliberate on new paradigms computing, cybernetics and intelligent systems developing international information processing infrastructures.
  • To identify key research findings and to propose new applications development.
  • To share prospective research areas and form specific research communities.
  • To publish state-of-the-art technical report and a set of recommendations for Business, Industry and Government sectors.
