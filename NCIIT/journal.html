Journal Proceedings

The accepted papers will be published in Inventive Research Organization (IRO) recognized reputed journals with additional
charge based on the willingness of the author.

Proceedings of Eleventh National Conference on Innovations in Information Technology (NCIIT 2022) can be published
in the regular issues of the following IRO international journals based on its thematic areas.

  • Journal of Artificial Intelligence and Capsule Networks (ISSN: 2582-2012) - irojournals.com/aicn
  • Journal on Innovative Image Processing (ISSN: 2582-4252) - irojournals.com/iroiip
  • Journal on Soft Computing Paradigm (ISSN: 2582-2640) - irojournals.com/jscp
  • Journal on Ubiquitous Computing and Communication Technologies (ISSN: 2582-337X) - irojournals.com/jucct
  • Journal on Trends in Computer Science and Smart Technology (ISSN: 2582-4104) - irojournals.com/tcsst
  • Journal on Information Technology and Digital World (ISSN: 2582-418X) - irojournals.com/itdw
  • Journal on Sustainable Wireless Systems (ISSN: 2582-3167) - irojournals.com/irosws
  • Journal on Electronics and Informatics (ISSN: 2582-3825) - irojournals.com/iroei
  • Journal on Electrical Engineering and Automation (ISSN: 2582-3051) - irojournals.com/iroeea
  • Journal on IoT in Social, Mobile, Analytics, and Cloud (ISSN: 2582-1369) - irojournals.com/iroismac

About

The aim of this conference is to provide an opportunity for experts from Academic Institutions and Industries to discuss and share their experiences, strategy and vision on the research activities in the field of Information Technology. Eminent Scientists and Engineers will also be invited to deliver special lectures. The deliberation of conference will be through presentation of papers and invited talks.
